Information

The Mountie Megabots are going to the 2024 First In Show World Championships presented by Qualcomm!.

After making it all the ways to the finals in the Hemlock Semiconductor division at the SVSU States championships. We landed 41 out of 514 teams in the state of Michigan, Which qualifies us for the worlds championships where over 50,000 people and over 400 teams from over 40 country's will attend

Worlds works similar to a normal competition (You can learn about how a competition works in the informational video below) except during worlds theres 8 competitions going on all at once and the winners of each of those competitions will progress on to the Einstein Fied where the winners will face each other in a bracket system to determine the world champions.

This page will be updated once the competition starts to show our match schedule and the live stream of our competition as well as the times in which you can watch us compete. In the mean time explore the videos below and learn about the robot and the competition.
